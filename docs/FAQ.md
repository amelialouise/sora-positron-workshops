# Positron + AI Workshops: FAQs

> **About SORA:** The State of Oregon Research Academy is a community-run organization welcoming all State of Oregon and Oregon University System employees interested in research and analysis. There are no membership fees or dues. Learn more at [oregonresearch.org/membership](https://oregonresearch.org/membership/).

---

## About the Workshop

### What is the Positron + AI workshop?

Positron is an AI-powered IDE for data science, built by Posit. The workshop is a 60-minute, guided, hands-on session for R and Python users exploring AI-assisted data science workflows. It runs in a pre-configured Posit cloud environment — no local setup required, just a web browser.

In the workshop, participants will:
- Tour Positron and its data science features. 
- Use **Databot** to import, clean, and explore a dataset. 
- Use **Positron Assistant** to write code, explain functions, and debug errors in R and Python. 
- Edit a Quarto document with AI assistance. 
- Publish a Quarto document to Posit Connect using the Posit Publisher extension. 
- Learn Posit's data science-friendly philosophy for AI. 

For full details, see the [Posit workshop overview PDF](./Positron_Workshop.pdf) or visit [positron.posit.co](https://positron.posit.co).

### Who can attend?

SORA events, including these workshops, are always open to all SORA members.

---

### Is this an agency-sponsored or agency-approved training?

No. SORA workshops are community-organized events _not agency-sponsored, not agency-approved, and not offered as official state training_. They are opportunities for personal, self-directed professional development.

SORA schedules workshops around times that work for participants, which often includes lunch hours, evenings, and other times when members may not be on the clock. Participation is entirely voluntary and self-motivated.

---

### Do I need to attend on work time or use a work device?

No. Many participants attend during personal time, e.g. lunch breaks, before or after work hours, or from home. **If you have any concerns about your agency's AI governance policies, we recommend participating on a personal device and on your own time.** That puts the decision entirely in your hands and keeps things simple.

SORA's focus is on personal-driven professional development. We believe motivated individuals staying current with modern tools serve their agencies and the public better, whether or not a formal training approval exists for it.

## IT & Agency Governance

### Does SORA have ITI or EIS approval for these workshops?

No. SORA does not seek, hold, nor manage ITI, EIS, or other agency-specific approvals.

---

### What if my agency has questions about the tools being used?

Agency-specific tooling questions are best directed to **Posit**, who work with public sector organizations regularly on:
- Browser-based development environments. 
- AI-enabled analytics tools. 
- Security posture and compliance considerations. 

Contact Posit at [posit.co/contact](https://posit.co/contact/) or through your organization's account team. SORA doesn't mediate these conversations.
